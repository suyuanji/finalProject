Casualties of Wars

The design wants to portray the soldiers involved

in various wars over different time periods.

The map is interactive and shows the number of the casualty

in certain regions in ten-year increments.

With a computer mouse, one can scroll over certain areas

of the map to read a description of the war taking place in that region.

The brighter and larger the selected area is, the higher the death

count during that specific time and in that particular space.

This is a project from Northeastern MFA program.
